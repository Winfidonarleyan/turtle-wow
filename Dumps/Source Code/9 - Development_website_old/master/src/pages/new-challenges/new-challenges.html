Leveling Challenges

No matter which way you like to play the game, Turtle WoW offers exciting and thrilling challenges to those aspiring to be great heroes. Whether you wish to slow down and immerse yourself in the narrative or throw yourself into the fray with the opposite faction in heartpounding skirmishes - these leveling challenges enable you to alter your playstyle to your liking!

War Mode

A new Glyph that permanently flags you for PvP in return for certain benefits: +30% for all experience gains.

This glyph will flag you for PvP combat permanently! Players can opt-out War Mode challenge once at the glyph master in starting zone or additionaly at level 60.

Slow & Steady

If you really like questing and leveling, play in turtle mode, your creature kill rates will be set to 0.5 rates. Enjoy your journey and receive rewards every 10 levels.

Vagrant's Endeavor challenge

Join the Vagrant's Endeavor challenge and bid farewell to the headache of having to pick the right item stats! You'll be forced to wear nothing but poor or common quality gear until you hit level 60. Enchanting items is not permitted.

But fear not, once you reach that milestone, you'll be rewarded with the coveted title of "The Wanderer" and a beat-up mule to lug around all your not-so-valuable possessions. Who needs rare items when you can have a donkey, am I right?

Hardcore Mode

Inspired by Diablo games and actual Hardcore Challenge of Classic realms, we welcome you to join the true test of your player's skills: leveling as a mortal being. The ultimate goal of this challenge is to reach level 60 without ever dying. Once youâ€™re level 60, you can participate in raiding and PvP without a risk of losing your character. Mode can be removed upon reaching level 60 even if the remove chance is already used.

Inferno Mode

Otherwise known as HC60, this is the max-level continuation of the Hardcore challenge! Upon reaching level 55, an attunement questline will be unlocked which allows players to opt-in to Inferno Mode. Upon completion of the questline, players will gain a [Inferno] effect, allowing them to continue their Hardcore challenge at max level, and remain part of the Hardcore ecosystem.

Exhaustion

You are a walking arsenal? Do you switch weapons on the fly, but are held back by lacking expertise? Choosing the Exhaustion glyph might remedy this problem for you! With this glyph in your possession, your rate at which your weapon skill increases doubles at the cost of no longer receiving rested experience. If you are everso restless and journey far and wide, seek the Glyph Master in your starting zone to enable this challenge.
