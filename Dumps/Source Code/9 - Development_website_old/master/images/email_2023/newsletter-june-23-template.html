Patch 1.16.6: Beyond the Gilneas Wall

Hello everyone! We are thrilled to announce that the development of Patch 1.16.6 is progressing at full speed. In case you missed our recent update on Discord, here's a quick recap of what you can expect from the upcoming content:

  1. New titles and challenges: Vagrant's Endeavor and Inferno Mode.
  2. New race and class combinations: Human Hunter and Troll Warlock.
  3. New end-game content: Lower Karazhan Halls (level 60 raid) and Emerald Sanctum (level 60 raid).
  4. New levleing zones and quests: Gilneas, Hyjal, and Gnomeregan Reclamation Facility.
  5. New rare monsters in the open world.
  6. New transport and flight paths.
  7. New dungeon: Gilneas City.

Stay tuned for more details about the upcoming patch. Please visit our work-in-progress changelog page to watch the trailers and read more details about the upcoming content!

Zone music & ambience

While 1.16.6 is on the horizon, we would like to provide some details about our music crafting team :) George Kouroupakis has composed several soundtracks for this patch, including the Emerald Sanctum — the official soundtrack for our new raid in 1.16.6, and Inferior Numbers — soundtracks for our new Gnome Hub, the "Gnomegeran Reclamation Facility." Additionally, József Kiss (formerly known as TU:RUL) has also contributed music for this update. He has composed Spectral Symphony, which features soundtracks for our upcoming raid, Karazhan!

You can listen to their latest and upcoming releases on our music profiles on YouTube, Spotify, Apple Music, and Deezer.

Fighting a good fight...

As you may already know, we heavily monitor all gold transactions so we'd like to provide you some numbers regarding our fight against any form of RMT, botting, and unfair play in order to provide you with a clean and bot-free environment. In the last 60 days...

  • ~400 gold-selling services advertisers have been banned.
  • ~350 accounts related to gold-selling services (mules, banks...) have been banned.
  • ~400 accounts of leveling bots have been banned.
  • ~150 accounts of cheaters have been banned.
  • ~250 player accounts that bought gold have been temporarily banned (and non-legit gold removed from their characters).

Please don't support individuals that are actively harming our server, the gold they offer comes in most cases from stolen player accounts. Be aware that if you are involved in these activities your account will be automatically flagged and eventually your ban will come.

Podcast about storytelling and game design!

For those of you interested in the lore and stories of our expansion, Mysteries of Azeroth. Check out DesignerDave's podcast, featuring our writers as special guests! While the WoW fan-based development encompasses a wide range of aspects, such as emulators, addons, and game modifications, this talk is focused on fan-written stories.

Dave was a Warcraft III development team member, where he focused on campaign stories and level design. As we aim to maintain the core essence of the previous Warcraft games in our modification of WoW, we hope this discussion will be of great interest to you.
