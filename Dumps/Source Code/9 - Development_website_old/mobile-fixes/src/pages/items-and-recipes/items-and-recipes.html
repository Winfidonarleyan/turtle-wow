Items & Recipes

Оne of our goals is to alter or enhance certain existing aspects of the game to uphold current standards, this also extends to items as well. Unique item design is one of the strongest parts of Vanilla, finding that item with the prefix "of the Bear" amongst the piles of rubbish has always felt good, feeling truly rewarded after getting that one item you grinded a dungeon for days. Even if we want to preserve this feeling there are items that are too weak for their qualities, that end up sharded whenever they drop.

Stats, balance and all that magic

Many crafted items, world drop epics and even some high level dungeon drops have been adjusted to bring their power level to similar items to make them more worthwhile to use.

Over 1000 new items have been added all around Azeroth, from simple quest drops to powerful armaments from dungeons and even some you can make yourself! Some items were contributed from the community via the forum.
