Roleplay

Turtle WoW is home to a lively roleplaying scene and roleplay has been an integral part of the server since its early beginnings. For several years, roleplaying turtles have been creating amazing storylines and character arcs. Today, Turtle WoW is home to numerous roleplaying guilds that host several events every week.

No matter whether you seek casual walk-up roleplay or an epic story, you will find it on Turtle WoW.

The forest rises

The ground trembled when Ancients, commanded by the Silverwing forces, marched upon Warsong Lumber Camp. A bloody battle was fought and both sides suffered heavy losses. In the end, the Horde was able to slay an Ancient of War and repel the night elf army, but at a hefty prize.

Smog in the swamp

After having tried to fool the Spire one too many times, <Blacktooth Grin> felt the Black Dragonflight's wrath when their encampment close to Stonard was ravaged by the black drakes. Only by mustering all of their force, they were able to drive the invaders back and secure their new home within the swamp, <Little Hordemar>.

Southfury Trading Co’s sunday caravans

The sunday caravans by <Southfury Trading Co> have become a regular feature of Turtle WoW’s roleplaying scene. The cross-faction guild runs caravans through zones all over Kalimdor, searching for profit and the best deals to be made.

Golden Moon performs stage plays

The traveling circus <Golden Moon>, currently situated in The Barrens, inspires and entertains the crowd with various stage plays. Pictured here is the debut of their hilarious comedy piece “The Love and Death of Arthas”.

The dark wedding

A shadow priest marrying a banshee queen - not something one will witness any day. Accompanied by the spine-chilling howls of the worgen of Pyrewood Village, these two dark figures vowed to not even let death set them apart.

The rangers on duty

With the High Elves still suffering from the loss of their homelands, the  <Heirs of Quel’thalas> are determined to uphold their people’s pride. After seeking refuge in the lands of the dwarves, the elves now rebuild and reinforce their ranks. After all, elven rangers are a force to be reckoned with.

Our team is dedicated to support roleplay!

Even though roleplay is - and should be - entirely community-driven our team supports the roleplaying scene wherever possible. This includes, but is not limited to, GM support for roleplaying events as well as guild housing. For all their roleplaying needs, our players can always count on <GM>Coun (Spuxx#8010).
